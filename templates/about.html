About ComplaintBox

ComplaintBox is a comprehensive complaint management system designed to bridge the gap between students and educational institutions. As a developer passionate about creating solutions that matter, I built this platform to streamline communication and ensure that student concerns are heard, tracked, and resolved efficiently.

This system provides a structured approach to managing complaints, with features including real-time notifications, status tracking, file attachments, and role-based access control. It's designed to be user-friendly, secure, and scalable.

Why I Created This

I created ComplaintBox to address the common challenge students face when trying to reach out to their colleges with concerns or issues. Traditional methods like emails or phone calls often result in lost requests or lack of follow-up. This platform ensures accountability, transparency, and timely resolution of student complaints.

My goal was to leverage modern web technologies to create a solution that is both powerful and easy to use, benefiting both students and educational institutions in creating a better academic experience.
